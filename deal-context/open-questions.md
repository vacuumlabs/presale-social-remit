# Open Questions

Questions that remain unanswered from the December 2025 discovery phase, plus new questions arising from the May 2026 re-engagement. Answers should be pursued on the 19 May 2026 call with Joseph.

## From December 2025 discovery (unanswered)

These were formally posed in [`team-inputs/2025-12-15-discovery-questions.md`](../team-inputs/2025-12-15-discovery-questions.md) but no answers were recorded in any available source.

1. **Target user** — Is SocialRemit primarily targeting UK-based migrants from eastern/western Africa?
2. **Product goals and origin** — How did the product idea start?
3. **Primary use case** — International remittances only, local P2P, or both? What is the differentiator?
4. **Business model** — Revenue via FX markup, fixed fees, subscription, or a combination?
5. **MVP corridors and currencies** — Which source/destination country pairs are in scope?
6. **Licensing status** — MSB registration, EMI licence, or operating under a third party's licence?
7. **Admin/back-office tool** — Is one required, and what capabilities (view, intervene, block)?
8. **Third-party providers** — Any pre-selected providers for KYC, open banking, card processing?
9. **Consumer vs business** — Consumer-only for MVP, or business accounts planned?
10. **Design assets** — Is there a visual identity, logo, or design system already?
11. **User validation** — Were the flows validated with end users? Any planned user testing?
