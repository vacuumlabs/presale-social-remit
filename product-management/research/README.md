# product-management/research/

Planned and completed user/market research for the SocialRemit engagement. Research
*briefs* (what we intend to find out, and how) live here alongside research *findings*
once gathered. Synthesised findings may also be promoted to `user-research.md`,
`pain-points.md`, or `personas/<name>.md` in the parent folder.
